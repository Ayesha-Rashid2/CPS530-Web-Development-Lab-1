The Majestic Peak:
Chongtar Kangri

This is Chongtar Kangri, most noteable known from it's nickname, The Majestic Peak!
Located between China and Pakistan, the peak is renowned for its treacherous climbing routes and breath-taking natural beauty, making it a magnet for hiking enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Facts about the mountain

  • This mountain lies in the Karakoram's Baltoro Muztagh range on the border of China and Pakistan, a region packed with extreme peaks
  • Its first ascent was made in 1994 by an Australian climber named Greg Mortimer, underscoring its technical difficulty and remoteness
  • The mountain is renowned for its steep, pyramidal shape and is one of the least-climbed major peak in the Karakoram
